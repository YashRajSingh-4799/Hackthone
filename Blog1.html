If we observe, we live on the only planet which can support life.The mother nature, had bestowed us with wonderful presents of lavishly green plants ,the beautiful meadows, the humongous mountains and the stupendous incarnation of god i.e we ,the humans beings. Everyone has their own identity no matter even if we are born twins. Our DNA cannot match 100 percent with someone other which proves that we are exceptionally different . Although, we have the same color of blood and nails but our mind,our soul,our perception towards our life and our thoughts ,is what isolates us from others.

Being optimistic is the talent that can't be stolen.Be it any field,if you know the trick of consistent behaviour nothing can cease you to achieve your goals.Its a fact that we waste our 80% of our life's precious time thinking about the presumptions that don't even exist.Don't you feel that its rediculous to instill our brains with useless thoughts!These thoughts had to do nothing except taking away some span of our miraculous life and making us deprive of hidden opportunities.Optimism,positivity,buoyancy are not just words they are the force that attracts health,satisfaction,bliss and offcourse success.Being positive creates an aura that reflects pious and positive attitude that lies within you.We have got one life and we don't value it.Actually its human tendency to undermine self possessions and praise other's.Somewhere we all feel that the great actors,economist ,CEO's have a vast source of income,comfort,luxury and juxtapose ourselves to those eminent one's and develope a habit of whining infront of others.This is one of the worst phase of human nature which is extremely useless.You have got one mind ,one life make its best use rather glooming of what you don't have. Also,it is scientifically proven that when you smile there is a group of "Zygomaticus major" i.e the 10 muscles required for smiling works and when you frown it requires only 6 of your facial muscles. So,be optimistic and revel into the world where you deserve to be jovial .
